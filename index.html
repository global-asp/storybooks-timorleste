Storybooks Timor-Leste

Storybooks Timor-Leste is a free open educational resource that promotes literacy and language learning in homes, schools, and communities. It makes 40 stories from the African Storybook available with text and audio in Tetun, Portuguese, and the most widely spoken languages of Timor-Leste. Share and enjoy!
