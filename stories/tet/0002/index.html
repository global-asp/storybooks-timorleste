Hateke ba animál sira.

Hateke ba animál sira.

Veja os animais.

Karau vaka dehan, “Moo.”

Karau vaka dehan, “Moo.”

A vaca diz, “muuu.”

Bibi timur dehan, “Meh, meh.”

Bibi timur dehan, “Meh, meh.”

A cabra diz, “mé, mé.”

Kuda dehan, “Neigh.”

Kuda dehan, “Neigh.”

O cavalo diz. “iihhh.”

Fahi dehan, “Grunt.”

Fahi dehan, “Grunt.”

O porco diz, “ronc, ronc.”

Manu-inan dehan, “Cluck.”

Manu-inan dehan, “Cluck.”

A galinha diz, “cocoricó.”

Asu dehan, “Woof.”

Asu dehan, “Woof.”

O cachorro diz, “au, au.”

To’os na’in dehan, “Shhh!”

To’os na’in dehan, “Shhh!”

O fazendeiro diz, “xiu!”
